Help

Dashboard

Shows your deposit balance and claimable yield per token. Refresh to update.

Deposit

Enter an allowed token address and amount (in human units, e.g. 100 for 100 tokens). You must approve the FundManagerAI contract to spend your tokens first (ERC20 approve).

Withdraw

Enter token address and amount to withdraw. Amount cannot exceed your net deposit balance for that token.

Claim yield

Claim your vested yield for a token. Only claimable (vested) amount is sent; the rest vests over time.

Strategies

Lists all strategies: target contract, token, allocated amount, harvested amount, and active status.

Tokens

Lists token addresses that are allowed for deposit on the contract.

Config

Set the deployed FundManagerAI contract address. Stored in localStorage. Reload the page or click Refresh after changing.

Fees

Deposit fee is deducted from your deposit before crediting your balance. Performance fee is deducted from harvested yield before it is credited to the reward pool or users. Both are expressed in basis points (bps); 100 bps = 1%.

Vesting

Yield credited to you vests linearly over a block range. Use Claim yield to withdraw the portion that has already vested. Unvested yield cannot be claimed until the vesting period completes.

Strategies

Each strategy has a target contract and a token. The manager allocates deposits to strategies up to a cap (percentage of total deposits for that token). Harvested yield is distributed according to contract logic (e.g. performance fee to treasury, rest to users).

Console API

In the browser console you can use: twizzler.connect(), twizzler.loadStats(), twizzler.loadDashboard(), twizzler.reloadAll(), twz.shortAddr(addr), twz.weiToHuman(wei), twz.humanToWei("100"), twz.getContractAddr(), twz.setContractAddr("0x..."), twz.panelShow("dashboard"), twz.validateDepositForm(), twz.doDepositWithValidation().

About Twizzler

Twizzler is the web interface for FundManagerAI — a DeFi yield allocator that routes customer deposits across strategies for optimal yield. Use the AvaAI Python app for CLI access. Contract: FundManagerAI.sol. All values are read from or sent to the connected contract; ensure you are on the correct network and that the contract address is set.

FundManagerAI supports multiple tokens and strategies. Deposits are subject to a configurable deposit fee; harvested yield is subject to a performance fee. Yield is vested over blocks; use Claim yield to withdraw your vested portion. Only the contract owner can add strategies, allocate funds, and harvest from strategies.

Before depositing, approve the FundManagerAI contract to spend your ERC20 tokens (token.approve(contractAddress, amount)). You can set the contract address in the Config tab; it is stored in your browser (localStorage) and persists across reloads.

Twizzler exposes a small API on window.twizzler and window.twz for programmatic use (e.g. twizzler.connect(), twizzler.loadStats(), twz.shortAddr(addr), twz.weiToHuman(wei)). Open the browser console to inspect.
